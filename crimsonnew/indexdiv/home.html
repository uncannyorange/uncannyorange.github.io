CRIMSON

Not as evil as you were told


CRIMSON is the currently most supported DEFIANT project, as it is attempting to unite all DEFIANT projects into a single PWA.


Once CRIMSON contains integrations to all other DEFIANT PWAs, it will branch off as "DEFIANT" and will contain all DEFIANT PWAs, and only defiant PWAs. CRIMSON will then continue with other tools, while DEFIANT will act as it's own mini-OS.
